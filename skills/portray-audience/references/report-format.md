# Report Format

Use this shape for the final output.

## 1. Focal Target

- focal brand or product
- category
- geography or channel scope if relevant
- matching note explaining what exactly was analyzed

## 2. Coverage Summary

Provide a compact table or bullet list with:

- source type
- what it contributed
- evidence quality
- notes

## 3. Intended Audience Signals

Summarize who the brand or product appears to target based on public-facing positioning.

Good inputs:

- headline language
- feature emphasis
- price posture
- visuals or merchandising structure

## 4. Observed Audience Clusters

List the main audience clusters supported by evidence.

Preferred shape:

```md
### 1. Cluster name

- Why this cluster exists:
- Motivation:
- Trust threshold:
- Price sensitivity:
- Confidence:
```

## 5. Motivations And Objections

For each important cluster include:

- strongest motivation
- strongest objection
- what proof or message matters most
- direct evidence attached below

## 6. Weak-Fit Or Excluded Audiences

State which adjacent audiences appear underserved, unconvinced, or mismatched.

Examples:

- advanced users likely to find the offer under-specced
- budget shoppers likely to resist premium framing
- gift buyers likely to need stronger simplicity cues

## 7. Commercial Judgments

This section is required when the evidence supports business-level conclusions.

Preferred shape:

```md
- Judgment: The clearest audience fit is cautious first-time buyers, not expert enthusiasts.
  Basis: The page emphasizes reassurance, simplicity, and visible trust proof more than technical differentiation.
  Why it matters: Messaging and offer design should optimize clarity and confidence rather than trying to sound advanced.
```

Every judgment must be tied to the audience evidence.

## 8. Confidence And Gaps

State:

- thin evidence areas
- hypotheses versus observed patterns
- weak demographic certainty
- channel limitations

## 9. Suggested Next Move

Recommend one next step only, for example:

- compare audience fit against two competitors
- run a bad-review pass to deepen objection mapping
- rewrite the hero section for the strongest audience cluster
