# Source Adapters

Use this file when deciding how to collect evidence from different channels and how to explain source limitations.

## Amazon

- Prefer product pages with visible review sections and sort or filter controls.
- Distinguish product complaints from seller, delivery, or packaging complaints.
- Treat one-star and two-star reviews as strong complaint candidates, but do not ignore clear complaints in higher-rated reviews.
- If the page is inaccessible, fall back to search results that surface public snippets or third-party summaries, and mark the evidence as weaker.

## Specialty Retailers

- Vertical-specific retailers can be strong sources when they expose cleaner review text than large marketplaces.
- Look for repeated functional failures, sizing complaints, and durability issues.
- Keep track of whether the complaint is about the product or about the buyer misunderstanding the intended use.
- Treat the retailer as category-specific context, not as a default channel for every industry.

## Walmart

- Walmart often exposes visible review text on public product pages.
- Watch for repeated retail copy or syndicated reviews; do not over-count near-duplicates.
- Separate store fulfillment problems from the product complaint itself.

## Reddit

- Use Reddit for language richness, complaint nuance, and category-level objections.
- Treat Reddit as lower-structure evidence than retailer reviews.
- Prefer firsthand complaint threads over generic recommendation lists.
- When discussing Reddit evidence, note that it is self-selected and not rating-normalized.

## TikTok

- Use TikTok only when the complaint is visible in public captions, overlays, or comments that clearly reference the product.
- Treat TikTok as supporting evidence, not the only evidence source.
- Avoid over-weighting viral anecdotes.

## Brand Or Retailer Review Pages

- These can be strong sources when reviews are public and product-specific.
- Be careful with moderation bias and survivorship bias.
- If the page has only testimonials or lacks visible negative content, say so rather than inferring positivity.

## Unknown Or Weak Sources

- If a source is noisy, scraped by a third party, or only exposes snippets, downgrade evidence quality.
- If product matching is weak, keep the note but do not let it dominate the final conclusions.
